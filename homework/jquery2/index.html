Maldives: Tropical Paradise

An island country located in the Indian Ocean, The Maldives is one of the world's most geographically dispersed countries. It's beautiful landscape is characterized by the tropical monsoon climate, which averages a temperature of approximately 28 degrees celsius throughout the year. The islands are filled with white sandy beaches and crystal clear oceans, making you feel like you're in a tropical paradise. Here are some places of interest:

  • Veligandu Beach
  • Maldive Victory
  • Alimatha Island
  • Manta Point
  • Hulhumale Mosque
